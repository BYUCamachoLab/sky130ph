Photonic Components#

adiabatic_bend_90#

sky130ph.components.adiabatic_bend_90(radius: float = 1) → gdsfactory.component.Component#

Returns adiabatic bend 90 degrees.add().

Args:

radius: bend radius. (From [1, 2, 3, 4, 5])
